GREENHOUSE INSTALLATION

At E-Plus, we specialize in designing and installing advanced greenhouse systems that maximize crop yield, enhance efficiency, and ensure sustainable farming. Our solutions combine modern technology with durable infrastructure, providing growers with fully automated environments that support year-round cultivation of vegetables, leafy greens, and other crops.

OUR GREENHOUSE SOLUTIONS

Vertical Circular Tower Installation

Space-efficient towers that maximize crop density while ensuring easy maintenance and harvesting.

A-Frame Installation

Reliable and scalable A-frame systems ideal for large-scale production and versatile crop growth.

Zip Grow Tower Installation

Innovative hydroponic growing systems designed to enhance yield and optimize resource usage.

Automation for Climate & Fertigation

Smart automation regulating irrigation, nutrient delivery, and climate to achieve ideal growing conditions.

Fully Automated Environmental Controls

Integrated systems managing light, temperature, humidity, and CO₂ for optimal crop growth.

Water Treatment & Cooling Systems

Advanced filtration through R.O. systems and chillers to deliver clean, temperature-controlled water.

Greenhouse Structure

Hot-dip galvanized steel frames with polycarbonate covering, shading, and temperature/light control.

Processing Room Installation

Post-harvest facilities for cutting, bubble washing, weighing, conveying, and packaging produce efficiently.
