About Us

Building Excellence Through Complete
Fit-Out & MEP Solutions

E PLUS EMC

E Plus, a Dubai-registered company, is licensed to deliver comprehensive Interior Fit–Outs and MEP services. EPLUS is specialized in delivering special projects in agriculture say high end vertical farming and green house installations including but not limited to warehouse construction, shade installations, complete MEP systems such as lighting, HVAC, Dehumidifiers, fire and life safety, Irrigation systems including fertigation unit and high end automation for the farms.

Our approach goes beyond project execution — we focus on delivering end-to-end solutions that support the entire life cycle of a building. By ensuring quality, efficiency, and innovation, we provide systems that require minimal maintenance while maximizing long-term value for our clients.

At E Plus, we combine technical expertise with a customer-first approach, positioning ourselves as a trusted partner for sustainable and reliable infrastructure solutions.
